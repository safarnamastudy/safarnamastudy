Admission Process 2026

The admission process for Data Science Certification generally involves the following steps:

  • Step 1: Application Form: Fill out the application form for the respective entrance exam (e.g., JEE, CAT, NEET).
  • Step 2: Entrance Exam: Appear for the exam and secure a valid score.
  • Step 3: Counseling: Participate in the counseling process (JoSAA, MCC, etc.) for seat allocation.
  • Step 4: Document Verification: Verify academic documents and pay the admission fee.

Direct admission is also available in some private institutions under management quota.
