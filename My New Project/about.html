MR. Aman Anand

Founder, Safarnama

At just 18 years old, Aman Anand embodies the spirit of entrepreneurship and determination. Hailing from a small village in Bihar, Aman has nurtured the dream of becoming a successful entrepreneur since childhood. Despite the challenges of limited resources and opportunities in his rural background, his unwavering passion for innovation and education drove him to create Safarnama—a platform designed to democratize access to educational information across India.

Aman's journey is a testament to the power of dreams and perseverance. Growing up in an environment where quality educational guidance was scarce, he experienced firsthand the struggles students face in navigating India's complex academic landscape. This personal experience became the foundation of Safarnama, where he channels his vision to empower millions of aspiring students with verified data, institutional rankings, and career guidance.

As a young entrepreneur from rural Bihar, Aman represents the new generation of Indian innovators who are breaking barriers and creating opportunities not just for themselves, but for countless others. His story inspires us to believe that no dream is too big, and no background is too humble to achieve greatness. Through Safarnama, Aman is not just building a platform—he is building a movement that transforms aspirations into achievements.

About Us

About Safarnama

Empowering Aspirants through Clarity and Data.

Welcome to Safarnama, a dedicated digital platform designed to simplify the complex world of Indian education and competitive examinations. In an era where information is abundant but often fragmented, Safarnama serves as a centralized hub for students, parents, and educators to find verified, real-time data on their academic journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To democratize access to educational information. Whether it is a prestigious government examination or a niche university course, we believe every student deserves a clear roadmap. We aim to eliminate confusion by providing structured insights into exam schedules, institutional rankings, and career opportunities.

What We Offer

Safarnama is built on the pillars of research and transparency. Our platform offers:

  • Government Examinations: UPSC, SSC, state-level exams with notifications, syllabus, and eligibility.
  • Institutional Rankings: Data-driven analysis of colleges and universities across India.
  • Course Discovery: Structured course insights aligned with career outcomes.

Why Safarnama?

  • Student-Centric Approach: Built by understanding real aspirant challenges.
  • Verified Data: Information sourced from official portals and gazettes.
  • Simple & Accessible: Clean interface with fast navigation.

Our Commitment

As India's educational ecosystem evolves, Safarnama evolves with it. We continuously expand our database, refine ranking methodologies, and improve accuracy to give aspirants a competitive edge.

Join Our Journey

Education is a marathon, not a sprint. Safarnama is here to walk that journey with you — offering clarity, data, and direction at every step.
