Management is the process of planning, organizing, leading, and controlling an organization's resources to achieve specific goals. It involves decision-making, strategy formulation, and effective utilization of human and material resources. Management education prepares students for leadership roles in various sectors including corporate, finance, marketing, human resources, and entrepreneurship.

A career in management offers diverse opportunities across industries, with roles ranging from business analysts and marketing managers to CEOs and entrepreneurs. Management graduates are highly sought after by top companies worldwide.
