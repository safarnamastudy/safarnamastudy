Engineering Entrance Exams

National Level

JEE Main

Joint Entrance Examination for admission to NITs, IIITs, and other central/state funded technical institutes.

Frequency: 2x/Year Jan & Apr
Advanced

JEE Advanced

The gateway to the prestigious IITs. Only top rankers of JEE Main are eligible to appear.

Eligibility: Top JEE Main May/June
Regional

BITSAT

Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test for BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses.

Mode: Online May/June
State Level

MHT CET

Maharashtra Common Entrance Test for engineering and pharmacy admissions in Maharashtra.

State: Maharashtra May

Medical & Science Exams

Medical

NEET UG

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for MBBS, BDS, and other medical courses in India.

Mode: Offline May First Sunday
Research

IISER IAT

IISER Aptitude Test for admission to 5-year BS-MS dual degree program at IISERs.

Focus: Basic Sciences June
Medical PG

NEET PG

Entrance examination for MD/MS and postgraduate diploma courses.

Level: Postgraduate March

Management & Law

Management

CAT

Common Admission Test for admission to IIMs and other top business schools in India.

Key: Top IIMs November
Management

XAT

Xavier Aptitude Test conducted by XLRI Jamshedpur for management admissions.

Difficulty: High January
Law

CLAT

Common Law Admission Test for admission to 22 National Law Universities (NLUs).

Course: LLB/LLM December

Government & Civil Services

UPSC CSE

Union Public Service Commission

Civil Services Examination for IAS, IPS, IFS, and other central services. The toughest exam in India.

Role: Grade A Officer Prelims: May
SSC CGL

Staff Selection Commission

Combined Graduate Level Exam for Group B and C posts in various ministries.

Role: Inspector/Asst Sep/Oct
RRB NTPC

Railway Recruitment Board

Non-Technical Popular Categories exam for Station Master, Clerk, and other posts.

Vacancies: High Varies

Defence & Banking

NDA / CDS

National Defence Academy

Join the Army, Navy, or Air Force as a commissioned officer.

Entry: 10+2 / Grad Apr & Sep
IBPS PO

Institute of Banking Personnel

Probationary Officer exam for recruitment in public sector banks.

Sector: Banking October
SBI PO

State Bank of India

Independent recruitment exam for Probationary Officers in SBI.

Salary: High November

Private & University Exams

Private

VITEEE

Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Examination.

Institute: VIT April
Private

SRMJEEE

SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination for SRM University campuses.

Mode: Online April
Central

CUET UG

Common University Entrance Test for admission to Central Universities.

Scope: All Central Univs May

Board Exams

National

CBSE Board

Central Board of Secondary Education. Official guide for Class 10 & 12 exams.

Class: 10 & 12 Feb-Mar
CISCE / ISC

ICSE Board

Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations. Comprehensive syllabus info.

Difficulty: High Feb-Mar
State

State Boards

Information on UP Board, Maharashtra Board, Bihar Board, and other state exams.

Scope: Regional Mar-Apr
