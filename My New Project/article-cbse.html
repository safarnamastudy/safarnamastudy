The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has released important guidelines for Class 10 and 12 board examinations 2026. Students and parents must carefully read these guidelines to avoid any issues on exam day.

Quick Info

CBSE Class 10 exams start from February 15, 2026. Class 12 exams start from February 18, 2026.

Dress Code Guidelines

  • Students must wear their school uniform
  • Avoid wearing clothes with writing or complex patterns
  • Simple footwear without thick soles recommended
  • Remove all accessories except simple watches

Mobile phones, smartwatches, electronic gadgets, textbooks, notes, and any communication devices are strictly prohibited. Carrying these may lead to exam cancellation.

Exam Day Instructions

  1. Reporting Time: Reach the exam center at least 30 minutes before the exam starts
  2. Seating: Find your seat as per the seating plan displayed at the center
  3. Answer Sheet: Fill in the required details carefully on the OMR sheet and answer booklet
  4. Reading Time: Use the 15-minute reading time wisely to go through the question paper
  5. Writing: Write answers clearly and neatly. Use headings and subheadings where appropriate

Important Tips for Students

  • Get enough sleep the night before the exam
  • Eat a healthy breakfast on exam day
  • Carry all necessary stationery in a transparent pouch
  • Read each question carefully before answering
  • Manage time effectively - allocate time for each section
  • Review your answers if time permits
