Important Guidelines

  • Unique ID: Ensure you write your Unique ID and Index Number correctly on the answer script.
  • Stationery: Use only Black/Blue fountain or ball-point pen. Fountain pen works best for good handwriting.
  • Calculators: Allowed only for ISC Class 12 Mathematics (Casio fx-82MS or similar non-programmable models). Not allowed for ICSE.
