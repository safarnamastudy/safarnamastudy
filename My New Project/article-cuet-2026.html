The National Testing Agency (NTA) has released the CUET UG 2026 application form. Students aspiring for admission to undergraduate programs in central universities across India can now register at cuet.nta.nic.in.

Quick Info

CUET UG 2026 registration is now open. Apply at cuet.nta.nic.in before the deadline.

What is CUET UG?

Common University Entrance Test (CUET) is a national-level entrance exam for admission to undergraduate programs in central universities, state universities, and deemed universities across India. It is conducted by NTA and has replaced individual university entrance exams.

Universities Accepting CUET Scores

  • Delhi University (DU)
  • Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU)
  • Banaras Hindu University (BHU)
  • Aligarh Muslim University (AMU)
  • Central University of Hyderabad
  • Jamia Millia Islamia
  • And 200+ other universities

Eligibility Criteria

  • Educational Qualification: Must have passed or appearing in Class 12 or equivalent
  • Minimum Marks: As per individual university requirements (generally 50% for general, 45% for SC/ST)
  • Age Limit: No upper age limit

How to Apply for CUET UG 2026

  1. Visit the official portal: cuet.nta.nic.in
  2. Click on "New Candidate Registration"
  3. Fill personal details, academic information, and contact details
  4. Select subjects/domains and language
  5. Choose universities and programs (up to 20 choices)
  6. Upload photograph, signature, and required documents
  7. Pay application fee and submit

CUET UG Exam Pattern

Section IA - Languages (13 options)

Any language from the given list. 50 questions, attempt 40.

Section IB - Languages (20 options)

Any language other than chosen in Section IA. 50 questions, attempt 40.

Section II - Domain Subjects (27 options)

Maximum 6 domains allowed. 50 questions each, attempt 40.

Section III - General Test

75 questions, attempt 60. For programs requiring general aptitude.
