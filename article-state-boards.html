Every state in India conducts its own Class 10 (SSC/High School) and Class 12 (HSC/Intermediate) board examinations. Here is a summary of the exam schedules for major state boards for the academic year 2025-26.

Major State Boards Schedule

Maharashtra State Board (MSBSHSE)

Expected: Feb 21 - Mar 20, 2026

HSC (Class 12) exams usually start 3rd week of Feb. SSC (Class 10) starts 1st week of March.

Uttar Pradesh Board (UPMSP)

Confirmed: Feb 22 - Mar 9, 2026

One of the largest boards. Exams for both High School and Intermediate typically run concurrently.

Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB)

Completed: Feb 1 - Feb 15, 2026

BSEB is often the first to conduct exams, usually in early February.

Rajasthan Board (RBSE)

Expected: Mar 5 - Mar 30, 2026

Exams are generally held in March. Practical exams in Feb.

How to Check Results?

State board results are declared on their respective official websites. Usually, results are out by May or June. Keep your Roll Number and Mother's Name handy.
